Welcome to T A V E R N

Tracking Atmospheric Variability from Eruptions and Radiation using a Network of Satellites

Grab a comfy seat and let's figure out how space weather impacts our atmosphere. A playground for analyzing and visualizing satellite orbit decay data with respect to solar activity.

About This Project

The goal behind TAVERN is to support our research in analyzing space weather effects on our atmosphere. This website aims to provide various visualizations of satellite data and solar activity.
